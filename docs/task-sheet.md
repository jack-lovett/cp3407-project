**Software Engineering Project Assignment: "MyClean" App Development
Project Overview
Objective:** Develop a **website** , **desktop** , or **mobile application** for **“MyClean”** , aimed at revolutionizing
the booking management system for individual cleaners and cleaning companies. The app will be **free for
end-users** (customers) and monetized either by selling to service providers or through other revenue
models.
**Project Brief**
* **Target Audience:**
o **Service Providers:** Individual cleaners and cleaning companies.
o **End-Users:** Individuals seeking cleaning services.
* **Primary Goal:**
o **For Service Providers:** Offer a robust platform to manage bookings, track schedules,
handle payments, and streamline operations.
o **For End-Users:** Provide an intuitive and efficient way to book cleaning services, track
appointments, and communicate with service providers.
* **Unique Selling Proposition (USP):**
o Identify and implement features that make “MyClean” stand out from existing solutions,
enhancing value for both service providers and end-users.
**Key Requirements**

1. **User Stories Development:**
    o Conduct thorough research on similar services to craft comprehensive user stories that
       address the needs and pain points of both service providers and end-users.
2. **Modern Tools and Cloud Services:**
    o Utilize **modern development tools** and **cloud services** (e.g., AWS) to build a scalable,
       reliable, and efficient application.
3. **Team Composition:**
    o **Maximum Members:** 4 students per team (5 with approval).
**Grading Criteria for HD Mark (DK: see the actual marking rubric)**
* **Innovation and USP:** Implementation of unique and innovative features.
* **Use of Modern Tools:** Effective utilization of modern development tools and cloud services (e.g.,
AWS).
* **Quality of Research:** Depth and relevance of user stories based on similar services.
* **Functionality and Usability:** Robust functionality and user-friendly interface.
* **Documentation and Presentation:** Clear, comprehensive documentation and effective
presentation of the project.

**Starting Ideas and Hints for Students**

**1. Research and Understand the Market**
    * **Study Similar Services:**
       o **Examples:** TaskRabbit, Handy, Thumbtack, and local cleaning service apps.
       o **Objectives:** Identify strengths, weaknesses, and gaps in existing platforms to inform your
          USP.
    * **Develop User Personas:**
       o **Service Providers:** Solo cleaners, small cleaning businesses, enterprise-level cleaning
          companies.
       o **End-Users:** Busy professionals, families, elderly individuals, property managers.
**2. Define Core Features
For Service Providers:**
    * **Booking Management:**
       o Calendar integration, appointment scheduling, automated reminders.
    * **Payment Processing:**
       o Secure payment gateways, invoicing, payout management.
    * **Client Communication:**
       o In-app messaging, notifications, review systems.
    * **Analytics and Reporting:**
       o Insights on bookings, earnings, client demographics.
    * **Profile Management:**
       o Service descriptions, pricing, availability, certifications.
**For End-Users:**
    * **Easy Booking Interface:**
       o Simple step-by-step booking process, calendar view, service customization.
    * **Payment Options:**
       o Multiple payment methods, secure transactions.
    * **Service Tracking:**
       o Real-time tracking of appointments, service status updates.
    * **Reviews and Ratings:**
       o Ability to rate and review service providers.
    * **Customer Support:**
       o Help center, live chat support, FAQs.
**3. Identify Unique Features (USP)**
    * **Advanced Matching Algorithm:**
       o Match users with service providers based on preferences, location, availability, and ratings.
    * **Subscription Models:**
       o Offer subscription plans for regular cleaning services with discounts or perks.
    * **Eco-Friendly Options:**
       o Highlight service providers who use eco-friendly products and practices.
    * **Integrated Inventory Management:**
       o For service providers to manage cleaning supplies and equipment.
    * **AI-Powered Recommendations:**
       o Suggest optimal scheduling, personalized service suggestions for users.
**4. Leverage Modern Tools and Cloud Services (e.g., AWS)**
    * **AWS Services to Consider:**
       o **Compute:** AWS EC2 for hosting backend services.
       o **Storage:** Amazon S3 for storing user-uploaded files (e.g., images, documents).
       o **Database:** Amazon RDS or DynamoDB for managing data.
       o **Authentication:** AWS Cognito for secure user authentication.
       o **Messaging:** Amazon SNS or SQS for notifications and communication.
       o **Machine Learning:** AWS SageMaker for implementing AI features (e.g., matching
          algorithms).
       o **DevOps:** AWS CodePipeline and CodeDeploy for continuous integration and deployment.
    * **Development Tools:**
       o **Frontend:** React, Angular, or Vue.js for web apps; Swift/Kotlin for mobile apps.
       o **Backend:** Node.js, Django, or Ruby on Rails.
       o **Version Control:** Git and GitHub for collaborative development.
       o **Containerization:** Docker for consistent development environments.
